Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 12, 2026

GitHub Lens is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how our browser extension handles your information.

1. Data Collection

GitHub Lens does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or browsing history to our own servers. All data processing related to your GitHub repository browsing occurs locally within your browser or directly between your browser and your chosen AI provider.

2. API Keys and Settings

Your AI provider API keys (e.g., OpenAI, Anthropic) and personalized settings are stored exclusively in your browser's local storage or synchronized storage. We never have access to this information.

3. Third-Party Services

When you use the AI analysis features, the extension sends data (such as repository names, descriptions, and README content) directly to the AI provider you have configured. This data is subject to the privacy policy of the respective provider (e.g., OpenAI's Privacy Policy or Anthropic's Privacy Policy).

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the GitHub repository.
